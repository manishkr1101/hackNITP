About NIT Patna

The National Institute of Technology Patna, formerly Bihar School of Engineering and Bihar College of Engineering, is a public engineering institution located in Patna in the Indian state of Bihar. It was renamed to NIT Patna, by the Government of India on 28 January 2004. It is an autonomous institute that functions directly under Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India. It is one of the 31 National Institutes of Technology in India and is an institute of national importance.

F.A.Q

  • Do you have a particular theme for the Hackathon?

    Yes the Hackathon is based on the list of problem statements. You can use any technology to build your hacks.

  • Are there any registration charges?

    Yes. There are registration charges.

  • Do you provide accommodation and fooding?

    Yes. Each team member will be provided with accommodation and fooding during the Hackathon. You don't need to pay a single penny. That's on us.

  • Do you provide travel reimbursements?

    No. There will be no travel reimbursement.

  • Is the selection per-member or per-team basis?

    The selection is a per-team basis.

  • I don't have a CSE background. Can I still participate?

    Yes. Creativity is independent of your technical skills. You can learn the tools and technologies anytime.

  • Do we get any certificate for participation?

    Yes, you do get a certificate of participation if you qualify for the finals after the initial shortlisting.

  • I don't find my question listed here. What can I do?

    You can always contact us.
